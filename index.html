Some mornings may not have the best start. So when you’re shopping for your daily commuter, choose one that always starts strong. Every Certified Pre-Owned vehicle goes through a rigorous selection process, where only vehicles that are under 6 years old and have fewer than 75,000 miles make the cut. They also have to pass through a detailed 172-point inspection where we check the inside and the outside; from the air conditioner to the power windows, the windshield wipers to the stereo. Now that’s a great beginning for a perfect ride, every day.

Get a Bumper-to-Bumper Limited Warranty for 12 months/12,000 miles† that’s an extension to the New Car Warranty and covers almost evert part in your vehicle. And a transferable Powertrain Limited Warranty† for a total of 6 years/100,000 miles that includes Courtesy Transportation.† Accepted at any Chevrolet, Buick or GMC dealership whether you’re going from the Midwest to Arizona or anywhere else across the nation. So if your journey takes an unexpected turn, you won’t be on the edge of your seat wondering what you’ll do next.
